What is SplitLedger?
SplitLedger is a lightweight, browser-based expense splitting tool designed for groups of any size. It helps you record who paid for what, see each person's running balance, and calculate the minimum number of transactions needed to settle all debts — with zero server, no login, and no fees. All data is stored securely on your own device via localStorage.
How It Works
  • Create or name your group (trip, flatmates, event).
  • Add all group members — no accounts required.
  • Record each payment: who paid, how much, and who benefits.
  • Watch the live balance panel update in real time.
  • Follow the "Settle Up" instructions to clear all debts.
  • Export your full record as a CSV spreadsheet anytime.
Key Features
  • Real-time per-person balance with visual indicators
  • Debt minimisation algorithm for fewest transactions
  • Search & sort across all expense records
  • Full edit & delete of any past payment
  • CSV export for records & accounting
  • Fully offline — data stays on your device
  • Mobile-responsive, works on any screen
